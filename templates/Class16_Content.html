Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Juki Industrial Bobbin
    • Juki Industrial Bobbin Case
    • DBx1 sewing needles 90/14
    • Large Flat Head Screwdriver
    • Desired Juki Presser feet
    • Medium weight muslin
    • Desired fashion fabric(s)
    • Ticonderonga #2 Pencil
    • Pencil sharpener
    • Tape measure
    • Ball head quilter's pins
    • 1" small silk pins
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Fine-tip markers in various colors
    • 14” x 24” large L-Square ruler
    • Alphanumeric Pattern Paper
    • Needlepoint tracing wheel
    • Fashion French curve/Armsyce ruler
    • Fashion Hip Curve ruler

During Class:

  • Students must present their trued patterns for review and begin construction of their muslin mock-up
    • Mock-ups must have facings installed
    • Mock-ups do not require linings to be installed

After Class:

  • Complete muslin mock-up construction and make changes to initial patterns as needed.
