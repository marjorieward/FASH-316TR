Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Medium weight muslin
    • Ticonderonga #2 Pencil
    • Pencil sharpener
    • Tape measure
    • Ball head quilter's pins
    • 1" small silk pins
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Fine-tip markers in various colors
    • 14” x 24” large L-Square ruler
    • Alphanumeric Pattern Paper
    • Needlepoint tracing wheel
    • Fashion French curve/Armsyce ruler
    • Fashion Hip Curve ruler

During Class:

  • Students will work on finalizing their patterns according to the trued drapes
    • Ensure all patterns have the correct notation (cut quantities, notches, tailor's tacks, etc.)

After Class:

  • Complete all garment patterns and cut necessary pieces from muslin/lining/interfacing/interlining.
