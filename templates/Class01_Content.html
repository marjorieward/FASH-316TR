Before Class:

  • Complete the Pre-Quarter Assignment
  • Read and watch the content provided on Blackboard > PQA.
  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Medium weight muslin
    • 3-5 yards black ¼ twill tape
    • 12 inches white ½ twill tape
    • 14” x 24” large L-Square ruler
    • Tape measure
    • Ball head quilter's pins
    • 1" small silk pins
    • Fabric Scissors

During Class:

  • Applying HBLs and control lines to the dress form.
  • Prepping muslin fabrics for draping assignments.
  • Understanding the basics of draping.

After Class:

  • Sketch and design a dress with a waist line seam.
  • Develop multiple sketches for review during the following class.
