Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
  • Medium weight muslin
  • Ticonderonga #2 Pencil
  • Pencil sharpener
  • Tape measure
  • Ball head quilter's pins
  • 1" small silk pins
  • Fabric Scissors
  • Fine-tip markers in various colors
  • 14” x 24” large L-Square ruler
  • Fashion French curve/Armsyce ruler
  • Fashion Hip Curve ruler

    • During Class:

    • Finish draping the bodice front and back, pin proof, and photograph the bodice with the skirt
    • Truing the bodice and skirt
    • Truing and Balancing drapes
    • Drafting the sleeve

    After Class:

    • Finalize which dress design will be draped for the Waist Seam Dress assignment.
    • Develop front and back flats of your final look utlizing Adobe Illustrator, Procreate, or drafting pens on clean paper.
    • Develop sketches and ideation of the the garments patten pieces.
    • Measure the assigned dressform and determine what muslin pieces need to be prepared.
    • Snip, rip, and block all neccessary muslin pieces.
    • Properly label each piece of prepared muslin with your name, piece name, grainline, and dressform ID.
