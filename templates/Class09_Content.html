Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Matching pair of desired shoulder pads
    • Medium weight muslin
    • Ticonderonga #2 Pencil
    • Pencil sharpener
    • Tape measure
    • Ball head quilter's pins
    • 1" small silk pins
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Fine-tip markers in various colors
    • 14” x 24” large L-Square ruler
    • Alphanumeric Pattern Paper
    • Needlepoint tracing wheel
    • Fashion French curve/Armsyce ruler
    • Fashion Hip Curve ruler

During Class:

  • Completing the Jacket Patterns
  • How to derive facing and lining patterns from the jacket shell patterns.

After Class:

  • Complete and submit Draping with Shapes Assignment via Blackboard > Submissions by the end of day.
    • All drapes should be photographed on the form, trued, and pin-proofed. These images will be compiled with photos of the patterns, construction process, final garment photos, and final technical flats to create one multi-page PDF file and submitted to Blackboard > Submissions.
  • Finalize all jacket patterns:
    • Front shell
    • Back shell
    • Sleeve
    • Top Collar
    • Undercollar
    • Front Facing
    • Front Lining
    • Back Neck Facing
    • Back Lining
  • Finalize acquisition of jacket fabrics:
    • Muslin or fashion fabric for jacket shell and facing
    • Interfacing for facing pieces
    • Lining fabric (polyester or silk)
